About this place

Experience the pinnacle of luxury living at The Meridian Penthouse — a stunning 3-bedroom residence perched on the 24th floor of the iconic Meridian Tower on Victoria Island. Wake up to breathtaking panoramic views of the Lagos lagoon and Atlantic Ocean, with uninterrupted sunsets that paint the sky in gold and crimson.

The apartment features floor-to-ceiling glass walls, Italian marble finishes, Bosch kitchen appliances, and a private rooftop terrace. Guests enjoy exclusive access to the tower's infinity pool, world-class gym, and 24/7 concierge service.
